The Show

A summer that changes everything

The Summer I Turned Pretty follows Belly Conklin, who has spent every summer of her life at Cousins Beach with her family and her mom's best friend Susannah Fisher and her two sons — the brooding Conrad and the charming Jeremiah.

But this summer is different. Belly is different. And the love triangle that unfolds will change all of them forever.

The Amazon Prime Video series premiered in June 2022 and quickly became a cultural phenomenon, praised for its heartfelt performances, gorgeous cinematography, and incredible soundtrack.

The Great Debate

Team Conrad vs. Team Jeremiah

The show's biggest question: who deserves Belly's heart? Here are the top reasons fans are devoted to each side.

Team Conrad 🌊

  1. He has depth and mystery
    • His grief arc is quietly devastating
    • There's always more beneath the surface
  2. The first love connection is undeniable
    • Belly has loved him her entire life
    • Their history makes every moment electric
  3. He pushes Belly to grow
    • Challenges her in ways others don't
    • Their tension drives real character development

Team Jeremiah ☀️

  1. He actually communicates his feelings
    • No guessing games or mixed signals
    • Says what he means, means what he says
  2. He's consistently there for Belly
    • Shows up without being asked
    • Prioritizes her happiness genuinely
  3. His energy is contagious
    • Brings joy and warmth to every scene
    • The kind of partner who makes life brighter
